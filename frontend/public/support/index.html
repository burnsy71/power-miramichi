Step 3

Set it as your profile picture (temporarily)

Set the framed photo as your profile picture until election day, then switch back to your original anytime. Facebook makes it easy to swap back — your previous picture is saved in your profile photo history.

On Facebook (iPhone or Android app)

  1. Open the Facebook app and tap your profile picture
  2. Tap Edit Profile → tap your profile picture again
  3. Tap Select Profile Picture — it'll open your Photos/Camera Roll
  4. Your new framed photo will be at the top (it's the most recent). Tap it → Save

On Facebook (computer)

  1. Go to your Facebook profile
  2. Click your profile picture
  3. Click Update profile picture → Upload photo
  4. Pick the downloaded file (check your Downloads folder) → click Save

On Instagram (iPhone or Android)

  1. Open Instagram and go to your profile
  2. Tap Edit Profile
  3. Tap your profile picture → New profile picture
  4. Pick the framed photo from your Camera Roll (it's the most recent one) → tap Done

Other platforms

The downloaded file is a standard square image (1080 × 1080). You can use it as your profile picture on:

  • X (Twitter)
  • LinkedIn
  • WhatsApp
  • TikTok
  • Any account that accepts a square profile picture
